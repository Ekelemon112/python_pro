You can easily book your flight by visiting our Flights page. Enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and number of passengers. Choose your preferred flight, review the details, and complete your booking securely online.

Can I make changes to my booking?

Yes. You can modify your flight details by logging into your Travello Account and selecting Manage Booking. Depending on your ticket type, you may change travel dates, destinations, or passenger details (fees may apply).

How do I cancel a booking?

To cancel your flight, visit Manage Booking, select your reservation, and click Cancel Flight. Refund eligibility depends on the fare rules of your ticket.

How much luggage can I take?

Your free baggage allowance depends on your fare type and destination. Details are shown during booking and in your booking confirmation email.

What items are not allowed on board?

For safety reasons, hazardous materials such as flammable liquids, explosives, and certain batteries are prohibited. See our Baggage Policy page for the full list.

Check-In & Boarding

When does online check-in open?
Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and closes 2 hours before your flight. Log in to your Travello account or use the Check-In option on our homepage.
What documents do I need to bring?
Please bring a valid passport or government-issued ID, and your flight confirmation (printed or digital).

Do you offer special assistance?
Yes. We provide wheelchair services, priority boarding, and other assistance for passengers with reduced mobility or special needs. Contact our Customer Care Team at least 48 hours before your flight.
What should I do if my flight is delayed or cancelled?
In case of delays or cancellations, Travello will notify you via email and SMS. You may rebook your flight or request a refund through Manage Booking..
